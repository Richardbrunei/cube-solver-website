What is this?

Rubik's Cube Interactive is a web-based tool that allows you to visualize and interact with a virtual Rubik's cube. You can view the cube in both 3D and net (flattened) formats, capture your physical cube's state using your camera, and manually edit colors to match any configuration.

Features

  • 3D Visualization: Interactive 3D cube model with smooth rotations
  • Net View: Flattened cube layout showing all faces simultaneously
  • Camera Capture: Use your device's camera to capture cube states
  • Manual Editing: Click and edit individual stickers with standard cube colors
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

How to Use

  1. Choose between 3D View or Net View using the toggle buttons
  2. Use the Camera button to capture your physical cube's configuration
  3. Click "Edit Colors" to manually adjust individual stickers
  4. Switch between views to see your cube from different perspectives
